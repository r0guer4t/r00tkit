This handheld is meant to lower the barrier to hardware hacking.
Simple in its design and purpose. 
Superb at one thing because it neednt be bad at all things.

Technical specifications;
	Rockchip RK3328@1.5GHZ
	4 GB LPDDR3 
	32 GB microSD card
	ESP32s2
	5 in. LCD over HDMI
	2 USB 2.0 ports
	1 USB 3.0 port
	1 Ethernet jack
